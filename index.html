About

Why PluggedIn? This site was designed for videogamers, coders, and astronomy lovers. Find yourself within a supportive community to talk about what you love...your passion.

Our Mission: Created for users to leave their boring, sad life and immerse themselves into a new virtual reality with no escape, hence being "pluggedin."
